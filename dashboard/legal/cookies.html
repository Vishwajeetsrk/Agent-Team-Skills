Cookie Policy

Last updated: July 2026

1. What Are Cookies

Cookies are small text files stored on your device by your web browser. This website uses minimal cookies and local storage to improve your experience.

2. Cookies We Use

NameTypePurposeDuration
themeLocal StorageStores your dark/light mode preferencePersistent (until cleared)

We do not use tracking cookies, advertising cookies, or third-party analytics cookies.

3. Local Storage

We use browser localStorage (not cookies) to remember your dark mode preference and demo mode state. This data stays on your device and is never sent to any server.

4. Third-Party Cookies

When using the template preview modal, iframe content from built templates may load third-party resources. These templates are self-contained and hosted locally within the repository.

5. Managing Cookies

You can clear localStorage and cookies through your browser settings. Disabling cookies may affect theme persistence but will not break core functionality.

This policy may be updated. Check the date at the top for the latest revision.
