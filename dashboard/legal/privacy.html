Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 2026

1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy explains how Agent Team Skills ("we," "our," or "us") collects, uses, and protects your information when you use our website and AI agent tools.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Information You Provide

We collect information you voluntarily provide, including prompts, project descriptions, feedback, and communications when using our tools.

2.2 Automatically Collected Information

We may collect technical data such as browser type, device information, and usage patterns to improve our services.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Operate and improve the agent team system
  • Provide technical support and respond to inquiries
  • Analyze usage patterns to enhance performance
  • Maintain security and prevent abuse

4. Data Storage & Security

Your data is stored locally via our memory system (~/.agent-memory/). We do not sell, trade, or transfer your information to third parties. Git-backed sync, if enabled, transmits data through your personal GitHub repository which is subject to GitHub's own privacy terms.

5. Third-Party Services

Our tools integrate with AI platforms (Claude Code, claude.ai, Antigravity, Open Code) and GitHub. Each platform has its own privacy policy governing data handled within their systems.

6. Your Rights

Depending on your jurisdiction (GDPR, DPDP, CCPA), you may have rights to access, correct, delete, or port your data. Contact us to exercise these rights.

7. Cookies

We use minimal cookies for theme preference (dark/light mode stored in localStorage). No tracking or advertising cookies are used. See our Cookie Policy for details.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy. Changes will be posted with an updated date at the top.

9. Contact

For privacy inquiries, open an issue at github.com/Vishwajeetsrk/Agent-Team-Skills.

This policy is effective as of July 2026.
